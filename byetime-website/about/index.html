The Founder

Built From Experience,
Not Just Theory

Shi Kian Wee (Kiwi)
Founder, Byetime.AI

Byetime.AI was founded on a simple frustration: too many businesses were investing in technology that didn't actually serve them. Kiwi built Byetime to change that.

With over 8 years of experience across fintech, digital platforms, and technology consulting — including roles as Country Manager and Head of Business Development across Southeast Asia — Kiwi has worked alongside brands of all sizes to implement practical digital solutions that drive real commercial outcomes.

His experience spans companies like Fave and OpenMinds, working with clients across Malaysia and the broader APAC region. Byetime.AI is built on that accumulated knowledge: what works, what doesn't, and how to make technology genuinely useful for the businesses that depend on it.
